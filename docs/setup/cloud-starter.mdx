MindsDB Cloud is a service hosted by MindsDB. It contains all of the latest updates and provides a handy SQL editor so you can run your queries right away.

MindsDB Cloud Starter gives you a dedicated instance with a powerful GPU to experiment and ship your AI/ML project to production. This is pay-as-you-go, so youâ€™re not tied into a contract - you can start and stop your instance whenever you want so you only pay for what you use.

You can sign up for [a starter account at MindsDB Cloud here](https://cloud.mindsdb.com/register). Follow the steps below that guide you through the sign-up process.

## Create a Starter Account at [MindsDB Cloud](https://cloud.mindsdb.com/register)

Follow [this link](https://cloud.mindsdb.com/register) and fill out the sign-up form, as below.

<p align="center">
  <img src="/assets/cloud/cloud-signup-filledout.png" />
</p>

<Tip>
You can view the [MindsDB Cloud Terms and Conditions here](https://mindsdb.com/terms/).
</Tip>

Once you agree to the Terms & Conditions and click the _Create Account_ button, you'll be asked to choose a plan for your MindsDB Cloud account.

<p align="center">
  <img src="/assets/cloud/plan_table.png" />
</p>

Here you can find out what features are included in the Starter version of MindsDB Cloud.

Once you select _Starter_, you'll be redirected to a page to provide your credit card details.

## Use a Starter Account at [MindsDB Cloud](https://cloud.mindsdb.com/register)

After completing the setup, you can [log in to your MindsDB Cloud account](https://cloud.mindsdb.com/login).

Now, besides a demo instance, you'll see a dedicated instance.

<p align="center">
  <img src="/assets/cloud/dedicated_instance_off.png" />
</p>

It is initially turned off. You can turn it on with the Status toggle button.

<p align="center">
  <img src="/assets/cloud/dedicated_instance_on.png" />
</p>

<Note>
Please note that the amount of $0.90 per hour is charged when the instance is on.
</Note>
